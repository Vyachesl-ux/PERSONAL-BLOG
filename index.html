who am i

I am 39 years old quite young one in front-end developing area, but very motivated person. I am in love in programming and eager to learn 24/7 with fire in the eyes. I have started to study HTML since september 2020 and for now I have good understanding in HTML and CSS and LESS technologies. Also I am a bit familiar with Gulp composer. Currently I am occuputed in learning JavaScript. At the same time I am working under a website for my close friend. After JavaScript I am planning to start learning React and other technologies.

I have a good skils in Photoshop and Illustrator as well. Also quite well known Figma as a disigner tool for website creating.

Initially I am a civil engineer with Master degree from NTNU (Trondheim, Norway) who decided to change the field. I have been working as a road engineer since 2017. However, after almost 2 years of occupancy my working contract was over and due to very low working load I have not got extencion of the contract. I have tried to find a job as en road engineer within 1.5 ears, but with no success. So I have decided to change my profession to IT area and now my hobby can become to a real job.

I am getting a real pleasure from studing IT and results of it.
